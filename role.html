requirements

  • 4+ years of experience UI, UX, IA, and design
  • At least 2 years working in a product company
  • Someone who values efficiency and love getting things done- we’re a small team, so we need people who pull up their sleeves
  • You take pride in your meticulousness, but you don't let it consume you
  • You have good communication and collaboration skills
  • Proficiency in any of the common UI design tools (e.g. Sketch, Figma, Illustrator, Adobe XD, etc.)
  • Experience working in a fully remote environment with a distributed team (or wanting this to be your lifestyle)

    • Responsibilities

  • Improving our product in an iterative fashion
  • Conducting user research to identify design opportunities
  • Developing user flows, wireframes, and functional specs to communicate design concepts
  • Defining visual design systems, style guides, and best practices
  • Working with the rest of the product team to scope and implement your designs
  • Mentoring other designers in the team
